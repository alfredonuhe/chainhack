Welcome to Deadman!

This app is conceived for a person, the whistleblower, who wants to link his "life end" to the revelation of a secret. The secret is stored securely on several Nano Ledger devices, which are then distributed to a set of trusted keepers. The keepers can use the devices to check whether the whistleblower is still "alive" anytime. In case of "death", the secret will be revealed.

Please select your role:

  • You want to store a secret password on the device
  • You want to check whether the whistleblower is still alive
